Pea Rice

This easy Peas Pulao is a vegan and gluten-free dish thatâ€™s great to enjoy as a light meal or hearty side. Matar Pulao features rice and tasty peas with onions and aromatic spices and can be prepared entirely in a pressure cooker or in a pan on a stovetop.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15-ounce) can chickpeas, rinsed and drained

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped

  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander

  • 1 teaspoon garam masala

  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder

  • 1 (14-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.

  2. Add the onion and cook until softened and caramelized, about 10 minutes.

  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste, cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.

  4. Add the tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.

  5. Add the chickpeas and cook for 5 minutes more, or until heated through.

  6. Stir in the soy sauce, rice vinegar, and tomato paste. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, until the sauce is thickened.

  7. Serve hot pea rice.
