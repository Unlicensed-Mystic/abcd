Gajar Ka Halwa

Gajar ka halwa is a popular Indian dessert made with carrots, sugar, and ghee. It is a sweet, creamy, and delicious treat that is perfect for a special occasion or a simple weeknight dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds carrots, peeled and grated

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 1/2 cup ghee

  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg powder

  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds

  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the carrots, sugar, ghee, cardamom powder, and nutmeg powder.

  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the carrots are softened and the sugar has dissolved, about 10 minutes.

  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the halwa has thickened and is a deep golden brown.

  4. Stir in the almonds and pistachios and cook for 1 minute more.

  5. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, use whole milk ghee.

  • If you don't have cardamom powder, you can use 6-8 green cardamom pods, crushed.

  • If you don't have nutmeg powder, you can use 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg.

  • You can also add other nuts to the halwa, such as cashews or walnuts.
