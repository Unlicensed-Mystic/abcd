Gobhi Sabji

Gobhi Sabji is a popular Indian dish made with cauliflower and potatoes in a flavorful curry sauce. It is a hearty and satisfying dish that is perfect for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cubed

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced

  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced

  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder

  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala

  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  • 1 cup water

  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the cauliflower and potatoes.

  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

  3. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

  4. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute more, until fragrant.

  5. Stir in the turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, salt, and pepper.

  6. Cook for 1 minute more, until fragrant.

  7. Add the spice mixture to the pot with the cauliflower and potatoes.

  8. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.

  9. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

  10. Stir in the cilantro and serve hot.

Tips:

  • For a spicier gobi aloo, add a pinch of red chili powder or cayenne pepper to taste.

  • If you don't have garam masala, you can substitute a combination of 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander, 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom, and 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon.

  • You can also add other vegetables to gobi aloo, such as carrots, peas, or green beans.
