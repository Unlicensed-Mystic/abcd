Kadhai Paneer

Kadhai Paneer is a vibrant, tangy, deeply spiced paneer recipe that is perfect for enjoying all year round. Made with pantry staples like onions, tomatoes, capsicum (green bell peppers) and Indian spices, this bright dish comes together in 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup buttermilk

  2. 1/2 cup gram flour

  3. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder

  4. 1 teaspoon ground coriander

  5. 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  6. 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek powder .

  7. 1/2 teaspoon chili powder

  8. 1/2 teaspoon salt

  9. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  10. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds

  11. 1/2 teaspoon curry leaves

  12. 1/4 teaspoon hing (asafoetida)

  13. 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

  14. 200 grams Paneer, cubed

  15. 1/2 teaspoon salt

  16. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  • To make the kadhi, in a bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, gram flour, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, fenugreek powder, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.

  • Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, and hing.

  • Cook for 30 seconds, or until the mustard seeds start to pop.

  • Add the tadka to the buttermilk mixture and whisk until smooth.

  • Bring the kadhi to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes, or until thickened.

  • Serve hot with rice or roti.

Tips:

  • For a spicier butter chicken, add more red chili powder to taste. If you don't have rice vinegar, you can use white vinegar instead.

  • You can also add other vegetables to the chili chicken, such as carrots or celery.

  • Serve with a side of naan bread for dipping.
